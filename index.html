Money: $100

This is my reimagining of the TI-83 calculator game, Drug Wars, and is still in a very early (but playable) state.

Your goal is simple; earn as much money as you can by buying and selling a variety of drugs between various cities. The police are on strike for the next 30 days and the loan shark you borrowed your startup funds from is currently in custody, so you don't have to worry about them... yet.

Now. Get out there and build an empire!

NOTES:
- Each game day will take 5 real time minutes.
